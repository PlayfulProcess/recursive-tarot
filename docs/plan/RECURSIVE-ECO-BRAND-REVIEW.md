## Why a tarot research file informs the recursive.eco brand

The whole arc of that research is, underneath, **a theory of how meaning gets made** — and that
theory *is* recursive.eco's product. Three findings map one-to-one onto the brand:

1. **"License to create" — meaning attributed after the images, by many hands, no canon.** That is
   the literal definition of a recursive.eco *grammar*: an open symbolic system anyone can speak,
   re-cite, and re-make. The research gives the proposition an intellectual spine (Dummett's
   game-first historiography; Butler's *resignification*; Derrida's *citationality* — "a sign means
   only because it can be re-cited").
2. **Found-by-making.** Campbell's *discovered* (the pattern was always there) vs Butler's *made*
   (citation with no original), reconciled: meaning is **found by making**. recursive.eco is the
   workshop for exactly that — you don't author meaning from nothing, you re-cite what's at hand.
   This is **bricolage** (Lévi-Strauss): the maker as *bricoleur*, building with available materials.
3. **"The discovered erases the maker; the making credits it."** The Pamela Colman Smith erasure
   ("Rider-Waite," not "Waite-Smith") is the cautionary half. recursive.eco's answer is structural:
   **named makers, owned grammars, a recursive public** (Kelty) that credits the hand. This is a
   real differentiator vs platforms that universalize and anonymize contribution.

## Proposed positioning

> **recursive.eco — where meaning gets made, by hand, together.**
> A workshop for grammars: open symbolic systems you re-cite, re-draw, and re-make for your own
> people — and the maker always keeps their name.

Supporting lines, drawn from the research:
- *"Fiction is real where it is enacted."* (Seligman's subjunctive / Campbell's function / Winnicott's
  transitional space / Butler's performative — four names for one idea.) → recursive.eco is the place
  of **enactment**: casting, reading, journaling, building.
- *"Found by making."* → the tagline candidate that compresses the whole thesis.
- *"A recursive public"* (Kelty) — already in use; keep it as the governance/ethos line.

## What this sharpens vs the current WHY/HOW/WHAT mark

The hero spiral says **WHY: Sense · HOW: Recursive · WHAT: Make Belief**. The research *vindicates*
"Make Belief" (the subjunctive "as-if" is the correct register) but suggests the mark under-credits
**the maker and the many hands**. The current spiral is one continuous, authorless line — beautiful,
but it reads as *discovered* (a pure mathematical form) more than *made* (assembled by hands).

## Logo v2 direction (candidate — see the About page bottom)

- **Keep v1 live.** The continuous golden-ratio spiral is recursive.eco's recognized mark. Don't
  replace it; iterate beside it.
- **v2 hypothesis: the spiral made of many marks.** Render the same spiral as a *crowd of short,
  distinct strokes* — each a citation, no single origin — that resolve into one recognizable form
  from a distance. It literally draws "found-by-making / many hands / one grammar." A first candidate
  is rendered at the bottom of the About page (`spiral/spiral-v2.svg`) to react to and iterate.
- Future directions to test: a faint **hand/monogram** woven into the spiral (the Pixie-Smith
  "credit the maker" move); a spiral that visibly **redraws** itself (the resignification animation).
