Spread Builder — design your own layout

A spread is just a set of positions, each with a place and a meaning. Add positions, drag them where they belong (on a phone: press a card, then drag — the board won't scroll under it), and give each one a label and the question it asks. Export it as a shareable spread file, or cast with it straight away. There is no right number and no fixed names — more than one layout is healthy.
