Courses & Sources

Everything behind the cards in one place — the courses, the sources (the books and the people), the illustrated history programs, and the views that let you see the whole collection at once. We hold the history to the record and show our working.

“The wrong history has been given in every published work which so far has dealt with the subject… there is in fact no history prior to the fourteenth century. The deception and self-deception regarding their origin in Egypt, India or China put a lying spirit into the mouths of the first expositors.”

— A. E. Waite, The Pictorial Key to the Tarot (1910), Part I. Waite designed the Rider-Waite-Smith deck and still built it for divination — not on a false Egyptian pedigree but on the symbolism: “the true Tarot is symbolism; it speaks no other language.” Full text (public domain).
