- [x] **Q-TRUMP-RULE** — Tarocchino: you are NOT forced to lead a trump. Correct rule: **follow the led suit if you can; if void, you must trump if you can; only if you can't follow AND can't trump may you discard.** On the LEAD you may play anything.
