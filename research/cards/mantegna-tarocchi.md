# The "Mantegna Tarocchi" — all 50 engravings

Header. Fifty engravings, **not a tarot**: no suits, no number cards, no court cards, no trumps,
no game [@web_mantegna_wopc], [@web_mantegna_met]. They form a single ascent in **five lettered
decades** — E (1–10) estates of man, D (11–20) Apollo & Muses, C (21–30) liberal arts & sciences,
B (31–40) three Genii + seven Virtues, A (41–50) planets & spheres [@web_mantegna_wopc],
[@web_mantegna_cleveland]. Source of imagery: Cleveland Museum of Art E-series impression,
1924.432.1–50 [@web_mantegna_cleveland]. Italian label-names follow the plates as catalogued
[@web_mantegna_dailyart]. **"Changed from parent" is omitted on every card — there is no parent.**
